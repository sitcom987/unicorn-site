The Book

Rest Is
Resistance.

What if resting was the most radical thing you could do? Tricia Hersey — aka the Nap Bishop — says yes. In Rest Is Resistance, she argues that our obsession with productivity is a form of oppression, and rest is how we push back.

It's part manifesto, part self-care bible, part critique of capitalism. And it's exactly the kind of book that sounds obvious until it completely reframes how you see your entire life.

We're reading it, arguing about it, and then drinking wine in a beautiful space in Prenzlauer Berg. Very on-theme.
